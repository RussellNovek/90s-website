<h1>Pokemon<br>Hints</h1>

By: Sam Ruby, Russel Novek, Dylan Nadeau 3/8/2022




Hints

Pokemon being at it's base a puzzle and collection game everything is important from what things you buy in the shop to what pokemons you catch.
But one thing that could give oyu a huge edge is knowning what kind of pokemon counters what. electric beats water, water beats fire, fire beats ice,
ice beats earth and earth beats electricity.
